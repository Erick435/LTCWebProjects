How to use Mortgage Calculator

This calculator tool will help you estimate your monthly payments on a your mortgage, as well as the total interest accrual over the life of the loan. To calculate your loan cost, just enter the loan amount, interest rate, loan term and then click calculate. The calculator will then show you what you can expect your monthly payment to be, as well as what the loan will really cost you (principal plus interest).
